Aha! Maps · Bellinger

Systems Library · Aha! Map · Bellinger

All the CAMS maps in one place,
country by country.

Every causal-loop diagram on Neural Nations is an implementation of Gene Bellinger's Aha! Map schema. This page collects them — the maps, their accompanying stories, and the forecasts that grow out of them — grouped by the polity they describe.

Ta,
Gene.

Gene Bellinger — systems thinker, long-time custodian of SystemsWiki, and author of the Aha! Map — built the causal-loop diagram tool that every map on this site uses. The schema (nodes, edges with polarity and lag, loops typed as Reinforcing or Balancing, archetypes) is his. The implementations here are CAMS-specific; the grammar is his.

Without his decades of patient, generous, open work the maps below would each have required ten times the labour and arrived with half the rigour. Pardon us, Gene — we have used your tool to look at some uncomfortable places.
