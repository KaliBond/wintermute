Research Project · Apr 2026

Epiphenomenon@Trove

A structural analysis of threat discourse, institutional coherence, and civilisational stress — using the Complex Adaptive Model System and the digitised press / parliamentary archives of Australia and the United States.

1900–2026 8 CAMS nodes Five-scorer ensemble 55M+ Trove documents
Framework
CAMS
Eight institutional nodes scored on Coherence, Capacity, Stress, Abstraction.
Primary corpus
Trove · Hansard · NSS
NLA digitised press 1900–1954; Australian Parliamentary Hansard 2006–2025; US strategy documents 2005–2025.
Key metric
Shield Hypertrophy Index
SHI = Shield NV − mean(Lore NV, Archive NV) · alarm +3.0 · critical +5.0.
Validation
Five-scorer ensemble
Findings tested across five independent scoring passes; inter-scorer variance reported.
