The Helm–Hands axis. The executive (σ = −51.20) and the labour base (Affect = −4.60) form the most degraded pair: the brain cannot coordinate and the body is exhausted. This is the structural definition of a society that cannot execute even if it could decide.

Fast-loop nodes (Helm, Shield, Hands, Flow) average stress of 7.38. Slow-loop nodes (Archive, Lore, Stewards) average affect of −3.20. The fast loops are overheating while the slow loops are negative — the system has no thermal regulation at any timescale.

11. Implications, Risk & Opportunity

Strength

Diagnostic clarity and residual technical-security capacity. The SAI of 1.00 means the system does not lie to itself, and Shield (K = 5.0) and Craft (K = 4.4) retain modest capabilities. If institutional reset occurs, these nodes provide the seed around which reconstruction could nucleate. Argentina's technical workforce — evidenced by Craft's coherence of 4.0 — is a real asset, though one being actively exported.

Risk 1 — Executive capture accelerating institutional cannibalism

Helm (σ = −51.20) is the most degraded node and currently hypercentralised. The combination of hollowed-out coordination and concentrated authority means the executive consumes institutional resources — bypassing Archive, overriding Stewards — without generating institutional output. Each decree that circumvents normal channels further degrades the nodes it bypasses.

Risk 2 — Labour base approaching functional breakdown

Hands (S = 8.0, K = 3.4, Affect = −4.60) is at the threshold where the labour base ceases to reproduce itself institutionally. Forty per cent poverty rates combined with austerity-driven public-sector contraction mean the Harvesters cannot sustain the caloric load the system demands. Social unrest is not a risk; it is a structural inevitability at these stress levels.

Risk 3 — Mythic vacuum inviting authoritarian consolidation

Lore (σ = −48.00) and Archive (σ = −27.00) together represent a system where meaning has collapsed faster than memory. The 21-point sigma gap between them creates a vacuum that rewards whoever can fill it fastest. Historically, this configuration in Argentina has preceded authoritarian or hyper-populist consolidation — the Temple emptied, the King fills it with his own image.

Opportunity

The libertarian shock-therapy programme, whatever its social costs, has momentarily aligned Helm's abstraction with a coherent (if contested) diagnosis. If Flow can be stabilised — genuine single exchange rate, restored price discovery, inflation sustainably below 50% — the Agora could become the first node to reach neutral affect, providing a circulatory foundation for broader recovery. Shield and Craft stand ready to follow. The window is narrow: Hands (Affect = −4.60) sets the clock.
