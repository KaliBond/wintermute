Complex Adaptive Model of Societies — v2.3 Formal Specification

📐 State Representation

Society S at time t is the state matrix X(t) ∈ ℝ8×4. Rows are the 8 institutional nodes; columns are the four state variables [C, K, S, A] ∈ [1, 10] (integers).

Each node is scored on:

  • C — Coherence: Internal alignment and shared purpose
  • K — Capacity: Functional effectiveness and resource command
  • S — Stress: Internal pressure and systemic strain
  • A — Abstraction: Long-horizon reasoning and legitimating narrative

🏛️ Three Ontological Layers

The 8 nodes are organised into three layers. Effective coupling coordination between layers defines societal health. Decoupling signals crisis.

🟣 Mythic Layer

Lore (3), Archive (4)
Cognitive coherence & legitimacy. Maintains the narrative and institutional memory that binds society together.

🔵 Interface Layer

Helm (1), Stewards (5)
Executive conduit. Translates mythic legitimacy into material action and coordinates resource allocation.

🟢 Material Layer

Shield (2), Craft (6), Hands (7), Flow (8)
Physical & metabolic base. Produces, defends, labours, and circulates the society's material wealth.

📊 Node Value & Health

Node value integrates the four state variables into a single health scalar:

V_i(t) = C_i + K_i + (A_i / 2) − S_i    range: [−7.5, 24.0] V̄(t) = (1/8) Σ V_i(t)     (system mean) σ_V(t) = sqrt( (1/8) Σ (V_i − V̄)² ) (system dispersion)

High V̄ with low σ_V indicates a coherent, high-capacity society. Rising σ_V signals polarisation between layers.

🔗 Bond Strength & Mythic–Material Coupling

Bond strength between nodes i and j (negative-domain safe):

B_ij(t) = sqrt( max(V_i + 8, 0) · max(V_j + 8, 0) ) / 32    ∈ [0, 1]

The canonical directed graph places Helm as the universal hub with full Mythic ↔ Interface ↔ Material bridges.

Mythic–Material Coupling Index (primary leading indicator):

Λ(t) = mean B_ij over all cross-layer edges

Λ(t) is the single most important early-warning signal. Values below 0.45 indicate critical decoupling risk.

🌀 Coordination Phase Space & CPT

Coordination state: Φ(t) = (V̄(t), σ_V(t)). Four regimes defined by thresholds V_θ ≈ 12 and σ_θ ≈ 3.5:

RegimeV̄σ_VInterpretation
Coherent-Capable≥ V_θ≤ σ_θStable, high-function society
Strained-Coherent≥ V_θ> σ_θCapable but polarising
Polarised-Capable< V_θ≤ σ_θLow capacity, internally aligned
Crisis< V_θ> σ_θFragmented and declining

Coordination Phase Transition (CPT) at t* when Φ enters Crisis with all three conditions met:

dσ_V/dt > 0 (dispersion rising) dV̄/dt < 0 (mean value falling) Λ(t*) < 0.45 (cross-layer bonds critically weak)

⚙️ Dynamics

Node value evolution follows graph Laplacian diffusion plus external shocks:

V_i(t+1) = V_i(t) + α Σ_j B_ij(V_j − V_i) + ε_i(t+1)

Order parameters tracked across time: ξ₁ = V̄, ξ₂ = σ_V, ξ₃ = Λ. Stress is handled via external export or internal accumulation in low-capacity nodes.

⚠️ Failure Modes

Systemic failure = layer decoupling → CPT, signalled by falling Λ(t). Societies do not fail by single-node collapse but by severed bonds between Mythic, Interface, and Material layers.

Macro-Coupling Failures (low Λ)

  • Chaotic Fragmentation: Material layer runs without Mythic coherence (e.g., 1891 Chilean Civil War)
  • Regime Rigidity: Mythic layer captures Helm and freezes Material adaptation

Node-Specific Taxonomy

Helm Isolation

Executive fragmentation or capture — V₁↓, β₁↓. The coordinating hub loses authority or is colonised by a faction.

Mythic Decoupling

Narrative–material gap. Mythic layer remains high while Material collapses — legitimacy detached from reality.

Flow Collapse

Supply or currency failure cascades to Hands, Craft, and Shield — metabolic shutdown from the circulatory node.

Late Abstraction Collapse

A_i drops after prolonged S_i rise — long-horizon reasoning abandoned under chronic stress.

Shield Inversion

Praetorian turn — coercion dominates a collapsing Helm/Lore. The defence node becomes the threat.

Archive Amnesia

Memory collapse → policy incoherence and narrative drift. Institutional knowledge destroyed or suppressed.

🔭 Universality & Falsification

All societies obey identical (V̄, σ_V, Λ) geometry regardless of culture, ideology, or time period. Crisis = mythic–material decoupling, detectable ≥ 5 years pre-CPT.

Falsification protocol:

  • Cross-LLM correlation r > 0.7
  • Λ(t−5) AUC > 0.75 on held-out transitions
  • Universal ρ(s, k) < −0.3 across all datasets

"Societies do not fail by single-node collapse. They fail when the bonds between Mythic, Interface, and Material layers are severed — a process measurable years before the visible crisis."
