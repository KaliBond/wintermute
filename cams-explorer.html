Complex Adaptive Model of Societies

Societies are modelled as eight functional nodes. Four metrics — Coherence, Capacity, Stress, and Abstraction — track each node's thermodynamic state over time.

Coherence (C)Alignment & order
Capacity (K)Energy & resources
Stress (S)Entropy & friction
Abstraction (A)Complexity & info
Node Value Formula
V = 1.5(C) + 1.0(K)
    − 1.5(S) + 0.5(A)

High coherence and capacity increase value; stress degrades it.
