Operator Glossary

V_i
C + K − S + 0.5×A
Node vitality: the net health of a node, balancing capacities against stress, with abstraction providing a fractional boost.
α_i
K − S
Affect: net emotional or institutional tone. Positive = adaptive momentum; negative = stress overwhelms capacity.
μ_i
(C + A) / 2
Structural mean: average of coherence and abstraction — measures the node's integrative quality.
γ_i
A × C
Interaction product: joint signal from abstraction and coherence. High values indicate a well-organised, conceptually rich node.
Γ_i
A / (C + 0.001)
Abstraction drift ratio: how abstracted the node is relative to its coherence. Values >3 signal drift from functional grounding.
Σ_i
max(A−7, 0) × max(S−6, 0)
Sisu (resilient activation): non-zero only when both abstraction and stress exceed high thresholds — a signature of antifragile response under extreme pressure.
B_i
mean of row i of W (Gaussian RBF)
Bond strength: how coupled node i is to all others via Gaussian RBF similarity in CKSA feature space.
