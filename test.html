    Method note

    Ψ(t) = mean V_i across 8 nodes; V = C + K − S + ½A (canonical).
    Λ(t) = B̄(t) / B̄₀ with B̄₀ = 90th-percentile of the society's historical bond-strength series (per-society normalisation handles MARKER / CAMNATIONS5 / older scale incompatibilities).
    σ_i = (A·C)(K−S); A·C is "abstract coherence load"; σ < 0 indicates material deficit weaponised against high symbolic activation.
    All values are observed — no extrapolation or projection.
