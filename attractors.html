CAMS Civilisational Attractor Space

Each trajectory represents a civilisational type modelled as a far-from-equilibrium metabolic network. Lorenz parameters encode coupling sensitivity (σ), stress dispersion (ρ), and dissipation rate (β). Rotate to explore the phase geometry of each coordination regime.

X → Bond Strength (Λ, inter-node coupling)
Y → Rate Dispersion (Ω, coordination stress)
Z → Coordination Capacity (system health)
